The transportation industry in Cameroon is a vital sector that plays a significant role in the country's economy. According to the Cameroon National Institute of Statistics, the transport sector contributed 5.8% to Cameroon's GDP in 2020. The country's transport sector is highly fragmented, and there is a significant need for reliable intercity transportation services. There is a high demand for comfortable, safe, and affordable intercity transportation services in Cameroon, and Kemite Global Group aims to fill this gap.

In addition to the transportation industry, the meeting room rental industry is also a rapidly growing sector in Cameroon. Many businesses and organizations require meeting rooms for conferences, training sessions, seminars, and other events. However, there is a significant shortage of suitable meeting rooms in Cameroon, and Kemite Global Group aims to provide a solution to this problem.

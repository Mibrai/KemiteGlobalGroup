KEMITE MEGASTRUCTURE

African identity, authenticity and excellence

Founded on the principles of African identity, authenticity and excellence, KEMITE GLOBAL GROUP aims to change the paradigm and offer a vision of an Africa that produces value, creates and innovates in all fields, and is proud and dignified. One of the flagship projects of KEMITE GLOBAL GROUP is the development of megastructures in the major cities of Cameroon and in the capitals of the Central African sub-region countries. These megastructures are multi-purpose buildings that offer all types of services in one place. They are designed to meet the needs and expectations of various customers and stakeholders, from individuals to businesses, from leisure to education. The services offered by the KEMITE GLOBAL megastructures include:

Hotel Rooms

KEMITE MAGASTRUCTURE offers luxurious and comfortable rooms that cater to travelers and tourists who want to enjoy a pleasant stay in a safe and convenient location. They also have access to other amenities such as swimming pools, gyms and spas.

Playrooms

Spacious and well-equipped rooms that can host various events such as weddings, birthdays and anniversaries. They also have catering services that provide delicious food and drinks for the guests.

Shopping Center

Modern and spacious malls that offer a variety of products and brands for all tastes and budgets. They also have entertainment facilities such as cinemas, arcades and food courts

Health Center

Mini-health centers that provide basic health care services such as consultations, diagnostics and treatments. They also have ultra-modern laboratories that perform advanced tests and analyses.

Laboratories

State-of-the-art laboratories that conduct research and innovation in various fields such as biotechnology, renewable energy and artificial intelligence. They also have partnerships with universities and institutions to foster collaboration and knowledge transfer.

Offices

Modern and functional offices that provide a conducive environment for work and productivity. They also have facilities such as printers, scanners and photocopiers that facilitate office tasks.

Training Institute

Training institutes: These are accredited and reputable institutes that offer various courses and programs for all ages and backgrounds. They include languages, computer skills, entrepreneurship and leadership.

Conference room

Professional and sophisticated rooms that can accommodate various meetings, seminars and workshops. They also have audiovisual equipment that facilitate communication and presentation.

Librairies

Rich and diverse libraries that offer a wide range of books, magazines and journals for all interests and levels. They also have computers and internet access that enable online research and learning.

About Us

Mr Youssouf Mouliom
CEO of KEMITE GLOBAL GROUP

Mr. Mouanji Mouliom Youssouf Aimé is the CEO and founder of KEMITE GLOBAL GROUP, a company that specializes in investments in Africa, marketing, consultancy, legal advice, and international trade. Born in Cameroon in 1986, Mr. Mouanji grew up in a diplomatic family, where he developed an interest in international affairs at an early age. After completing his primary and secondary education in Cameroon, Mr. Mouanji decided to pursue a career in law. He attended the University of Yaoundé II, where he graduated with a Bachelor's degree in International Law. Mr Mouanji is graduated from the university of BESANCON in FRANCE . He obtain a Master's degree in Political Science from the University, where he specialized in Geo-strategy. Mr. Mouanji's academic excellence and strong leadership skills were evident from an early age. During his studies, he served as the President of the Student Union at the University of Yaounde, where he led various initiatives aimed at improving the welfare of students and promoting academic excellence. After completing his studies, Mr. Mouanji worked for several years as a journalist analyst in a radio and as legal advisor for multinational companies operating in Cameroon. During this time, he gained valuable experience in international trade and investment, which would later prove useful in his work at KEMITE GLOBAL GROUP. In 2018, Mr. Mouanji founded KEMITE GLOBAL GROUP with the aim of providing high-quality investment and advisory services to clients in Africa and beyond. The company quickly gained a reputation for excellence, and under Mr. Mouanji's leadership, it has grown to become one of the most respected investment firms in Africa. Mr. Mouanji's vision for KEMITE GLOBAL GROUP is to promote sustainable economic growth and development in Africa by providing innovative investment solutions to clients. His leadership and strategic thinking have been instrumental in the company's success, and he is widely recognized as a leading figure in the African investment community. In addition to his work at KEMITE GLOBAL GROUP, Mr. Mouanji is also a sought-after speaker and advisor on issues related to geopolitic, international trade, investment, and economic development in Africa. He has spoken at numerous conferences and events in France, Cameroon, Democratic Republic of Congo, Saudi Arabia, sharing his insights and expertise with business leaders, policymakers, and academics. Mr. Mouanji Mouliom Youssouf Aimé is a highly accomplished entrepreneur and leader who has made a significant contribution to the African investment industry. His vision, dedication, and commitment to excellence have earned him the respect and admiration of his peers and clients alike. With his continued leadership, KEMITE GLOBAL GROUP is poised to make an even greater impact on the African investment landscape in the years to come.
